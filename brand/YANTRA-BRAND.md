# Yantra Audio

Creative audio plugins.

## The mark
A Merkabah (star tetrahedron) — two interlocked tetrahedra — with ॐ (Om) resting at the bindu, the still point at the center of the spinning geometry. The core radiates light; the geometry passes in front of and behind it in true 3D.

## Colors
- Gold (ascending tetrahedron): #E0A85B
- Teal (descending tetrahedron): #4FD1C5
- Bone white (Om + core light): #F4F2EE
- Tile dark: #202529 → #131619
- Background ink: #0E1012

## Type
- Wordmark: Sora SemiBold, letter-spacing -0.018em
- "AUDIO" subline: Sora Regular, letter-spacing 0.5em
- Om glyph: Noto Sans Devanagari Medium

## Files
- Yantra Audio Mark.svg — vector mark on plugin tile (scales to any size)
- icon-1024.png / icon-512.png / icon-128.png — raster icons
- Merkabah Animated.html — the living logo: self-contained, works offline; open in any browser or embed in a webview
